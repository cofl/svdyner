Sul'va'í-dun, sul'va'í-dun,
wòn va í'hlæ'núl.
A'nreth, a'nreth,
wòn trel'úër qær-dun.
Sol'nreth-uòn va'ærð-kúq,
va-wòn luit eu æzu.
Æs r'é mun-núl kúd un eu
hla'wòn é wòn eu on dun...

Good morning, good morning,
it's time to wake up.
The world, the world,
the cities are silver.
The sun is high in the sky
and it is big and blue.
Go, you, play under light and
continue to exist and feel good.
