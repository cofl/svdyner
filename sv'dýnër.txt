 - "é r'é" can be pronounced "ay r'ah"
 - "r'é é" can be pronounced "r'ay ah"
rules:
 - plurals: ër
 - possessive: n if vowel, e if consonant
 - noun-descriptors are prefix' or -postfix
 
punctuation:
 - always end with .
 - ¿ for question at start
 - ¡ for exclamation at start
 - «» around concept/name
